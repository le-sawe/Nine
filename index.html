Nine Game

How to play ?
  • By turn, each player put a smily in an empty cell
  • left click to put a smile
  • if placing this smily makes 3, 6, 9 ,12 smilies to become aligned his score will be increased by the total number of newly aligned smilies(vetical and horizontal) an he will keep turn
  • Turn swaps when the placed smily brings no score to the current player (meaning that no smilies were aligned to from a total or 3 ,6 ,9 or 12)
  • you can restart the game by clicking the restart button
  • you can change the board dimension (width between 9 and 99) (height between 12 and 99)
Have fun ! :)
